    The problems are in order of increasing complexity. For m10 do 1-7; for m16 do 3-4.

  1. Ball hanging from a string: static, 1D, 1 contact force
  2. Stationary person on level surface: static, 1D, 1 contact force
  3. Accelerating person on level surface: dynamic, 2D, 1 contact force
  4. Stationary block on inclined surface: static, 2D, 1 contact force
  5. Stationary person on inclined surface: static, 2D, 1 contact force
  6. Stationary person on inclined surface holding rope: static, 2D, pulley
  7. Stationary person on inclined surface holding rope: static, 2D, 2 contact forces
